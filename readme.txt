A simple tool to get more customers via your website. An easy to use plugin that generates optimized location page with schema.org and hCard markups.

== Description ==

**Improve your local SEO with LBC Local SEO and rank high in Google in local searches!**   

**BECAUSE OF LACK OF TIME IT WON'T BE UPDATED AND SUPPORTED FOR INDEFINITE TIME!**      
    
LBC Local SEO displays business information in the way Google and other search engines need them. Provides a location page that is marked up with rich snippets (schema.org and hCard markups) in a few minutes.   
Displays essential information for your customers about your business. Information that help them to find and contact you quickly.

**Features:**

- schema.org and hCard markups (rich snippets) that help search engines to index the page
- editable meta description
- easy to use admin panel
- responsive design
- translation ready
- you can select which information you would like to show or hide
- business information (name, type, short description, website url, logo, etc.)
- location details (address, geo coordinates)
- geo coordinates calculation with 1 click
- contact details (phone numbers, fax numbers, email)
- click-to-call links on phone numbers to make it easy for your customers to call you, just 1 click to make a call
- mail-to link on email to make it easy for your customers to send you an email
- payment details (accepted payment types, accepted currencies, price range)
- opening hours (12 or 24 hour-format)
- special opening hours for holidays (12 or 24 hour-format)
- social media icons and links (Facebook, Twitter, Google+, YouTube, and more)
- Google Maps

== Installation ==

1. On the admin page click on the 'Plugins' menu.
2. Click on the 'Add New' button.
3. Click on the 'Search' link.
4. Fill 'LBC Local SEO' into the search field.
5. Click on the 'Search Plugins' button.
6. Find the 'LBC Local SEO' plugin in the result list.
7. Click on the 'Install Now' link.
8. Click on the 'Activate plugin' link.

OR

1. Download the installation package (.zip file) to your computer.
2. On the admin page click on the 'Plugins' menu.
3. Click on the 'Add New' button.
4. Click on the 'Upload' link.
5. Click on the 'Browse...' button then find the installation package (.zip file) on your computer.
6. Click on the 'Install Now' button.
7. Click on the 'Activate plugin' link.
